About Our Application

AI Blockchain Product Verification System is a smart security platform designed to identify genuine and fake products using Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain Technology, and Cloud Infrastructure.

Project Overview

This application helps manufacturers and customers verify products securely using AI-based image analysis and blockchain storage. Each registered product is stored securely with unique product details, image features, and QR verification support.

AI Verification

CNN-based image analysis helps identify genuine and fake products with feature comparison technology.

Blockchain Security

Every product registration is securely stored inside blockchain blocks to prevent tampering and duplication.

QR Authentication

Each product generates a QR code for instant product verification through mobile devices.

Cloud Deployment

The application is deployed using AWS Cloud, Nginx, Gunicorn, Docker, and Jenkins CI/CD pipeline.

Technology Stack

Python Flask TensorFlow CNN Blockchain MySQL AWS EC2 Docker Jenkins Nginx Gunicorn CloudWatch

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce counterfeit products in the market by combining Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain technologies into a secure, scalable, and modern verification platform.
